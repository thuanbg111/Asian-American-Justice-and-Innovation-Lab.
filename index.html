ASIAN AMERICAN JUSTICE AND INNOVATION LAB

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our mission is to address the need for the visible integration of Asian American narratives as an integral part of our collective history and the future of social change — through education, community building, the arts, and innovative programming.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

We amplify Asian American stories and leadership through programs that build understanding and spark social change.

  • Education & public programming
  • Community building & coalition work
  • Arts initiatives & innovation labs

Education

Workshops, curricula, and talks that integrate Asian American narratives into classrooms and civic spaces.

Community

Convenings and mutual‑aid projects that build bridges across communities.

Arts & Innovation

Creative projects, exhibitions, and innovative programs that advance equity and belonging.
